Hello, My name is Nicholas C. Maas I am a web developer. I am creative to the bone, having an interest in the arts as a child, I grew to love design, and enjoy the creative process. From the idea stage to the finished project, weither it was drawing, painting, building, I found myself lost in a world of possibilities. Where the outcome was fully realized, the vision came to life and flourished. Who is Neko Carrera? I used to play in a rock band under the stage name Neko Carrera, which is a portmanteau of my favorite singer Neko Case, and my favorite sports car the Porsche Carrera. My work ethic is that of the blue collar worker, putting in maximum effort, to achieve my goals. getting results in the alloted time, and delivering the highest quality product, available for immediate use. With the user experience in mind, along with a design thats guaranteed to turn heads.

The Blues and Brews search engine that I helped create with the help of my DU bootcamp classmates Andrew Karpisz, and Anteneh Negera. I also developed a day planner that is pretty sweet too.
