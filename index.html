Neko Carrera

Hello, My name is Nicholas C. Maas I am a web developer. I am creative to the bone, having an interest in the arts as a child, I grew to love design, and enjoy the creative process. From the idea stage to the finished project, whether it was drawing, painting, building, I found myself lost in a world of possibilities. When I realized my potential, and possibilities, my vision has come to life and flourished. Who is Neko Carrera? I used to play in a rock band under the stage name Neko Carrera, which is a portmanteau of my favorite singer Neko Case, and my favorite sports car the Porsche Carrera. My work ethic is that of the blue collar worker, putting in maximum effort, to achieve my goals. Getting results in the alloted time, and delivering the highest quality product, available for immediate use. With the user experience in mind, along with a design thats guaranteed to turn heads.

Neko Carrera has only been around a short time in the developer community. I have made a few websites worthy of your attention. Such classics as my Hockey Quiz, which I loved creating, the Blues and Brews search engine, the kick ass day planner, Nikki Carrera's burger shack, I helped develop the TravelTo app, and many more. More than just a front end guy(design will always be my jam), I have acquired an interest in the back end too, I love what I do, so don't be afraid to contact me with your project. Every project no matter how big or small will get my full attention, so that you will be satisfied with the finished project.
